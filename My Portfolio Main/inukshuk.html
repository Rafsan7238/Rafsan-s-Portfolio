Inukshuk Books

- Product Manager
- Coursera Capstone Project

This project is part of the Coursera Specialisation, Software Product Management by University of Alberta, and is a mandatory capstone project required to get the specialisation certificate.

In this six-week capstone course, I gained practical management experience in a safe, simulated software production setting. I applied Agile practices through the Scrum framework, and techniques to conquer industry-inspired challenges. Interacting with a realistic client, I discerned what they want and expressed what they truly need in software requirements to drive software production. Upon completing the capstone, I have been prepared to advance my career as a confident software product management professional.
